## 1.4 Data Security

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your data:

### 1.4.1 — Encryption

- **Data in Transit:** All data sent between your device and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS 1.2+ encryption
- **Data at Rest:** Sensitive data (passwords, payment info, personal identifiers) is encrypted using AES-256
- **Database Encryption:** Student records, financial data, and communication logs are encrypted at the database level

### 1.4.2 — Access Controls

- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC):** Teachers access only student data from their classes; parents see only their child's data
- **Password Requirements:** Administrators must enforce strong passwords (minimum 8 characters, mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, special characters)
- **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA):** Optional 2FA for administrator accounts to prevent unauthorized access
- **Session Management:** Automatic logout after 30 minutes of inactivity; secure session tokens with expiration
- **Admin Audit Trail:** Complete logging of who accessed what data, when, and from which IP address

### 1.4.3 — Infrastructure Security

- **Secure Hosting:** Data stored on ISO 27001 certified cloud servers with regular security audits
- **Firewall Protection:** Advanced firewalls to prevent unauthorized network access
- **DDoS Protection:** Protection against distributed denial-of-service attacks
- **Regular Security Updates:** All systems patched within 48 hours of critical vulnerabilities
- **Intrusion Detection:** Real-time monitoring for suspicious activities
- **Redundancy & Backups:** Multiple data center backups; automatic failover systems
- **Physical Security:** Data centers with 24/7 surveillance, access controls, and environmental monitoring

### 1.4.4 — Employee & Vendor Security

- **Employee Confidentiality Agreements:** All staff sign NDAs and are trained on data protection
- **Background Checks:** Regular security clearance verification
- **Vendor Assessments:** Third-party service providers undergo security audits before engagement
- **Limited Access:** Employees access only data required for their job function
- **Incident Response Team:** Dedicated team to respond to and investigate security breaches within 24 hours

### 1.4.5 — Limitations

While we use best-effort security measures, no online platform can guarantee 100% security. We cannot be held responsible for:
- Breaches caused by user negligence (sharing passwords, using weak credentials)
- Malware on user devices
- Unauthorized access due to third-party services (email providers, SMS gateways)
- Natural disasters or acts of God beyond our control

**Right to Restrict Processing:**
