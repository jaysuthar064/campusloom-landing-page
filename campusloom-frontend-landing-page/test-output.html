PRIVACY POLICY

1.1 Introduction

Welcome to SmartShala.

SmartShala is a comprehensive School Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system designed to streamline educational administration, manage student information, attendance, fee management, academic records, communication with parents, and provide analytics to improve school operations.

We understand that privacy is paramount when handling educational data, especially student and family information. This Privacy Policy explains how SmartShala ("we," "us," "our," or "SmartShala") collects, uses, protects, and manages information when you access and use our platform, website, mobile applications, and services (collectively, the "Service").

By accessing or using SmartShala, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use our Service. We will notify you if we make material changes to this policy.

1.4 Data Security

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your data:

1.4.1 — Encryption

  • Data in Transit: All data sent between your device and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS 1.2+ encryption
  • Data at Rest: Sensitive data (passwords, payment info, personal identifiers) is encrypted using AES-256
  • Database Encryption: Student records, financial data, and communication logs are encrypted at the database level

1.4.2 — Access Controls

  • Role-Based Access Control (RBAC): Teachers access only student data from their classes; parents see only their child's data
  • Password Requirements: Administrators must enforce strong passwords (minimum 8 characters, mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, special characters)
  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Optional 2FA for administrator accounts to prevent unauthorized access
  • Session Management: Automatic logout after 30 minutes of inactivity; secure session tokens with expiration
  • Admin Audit Trail: Complete logging of who accessed what data, when, and from which IP address

1.4.3 — Infrastructure Security

  • Secure Hosting: Data stored on ISO 27001 certified cloud servers with regular security audits
  • Firewall Protection: Advanced firewalls to prevent unauthorized network access
  • DDoS Protection: Protection against distributed denial-of-service attacks
  • Regular Security Updates: All systems patched within 48 hours of critical vulnerabilities
  • Intrusion Detection: Real-time monitoring for suspicious activities
  • Redundancy & Backups: Multiple data center backups; automatic failover systems
  • Physical Security: Data centers with 24/7 surveillance, access controls, and environmental monitoring

1.4.4 — Employee & Vendor Security

  • Employee Confidentiality Agreements: All staff sign NDAs and are trained on data protection
  • Background Checks: Regular security clearance verification
  • Vendor Assessments: Third-party service providers undergo security audits before engagement
  • Limited Access: Employees access only data required for their job function
  • Incident Response Team: Dedicated team to respond to and investigate security breaches within 24 hours

1.4.5 — Limitations

While we use best-effort security measures, no online platform can guarantee 100% security. We cannot be held responsible for:

  • Breaches caused by user negligence (sharing passwords, using weak credentials)
  • Malware on user devices
  • Unauthorized access due to third-party services (email providers, SMS gateways)
  • Natural disasters or acts of God beyond our control

1.6 User Rights & Data Control

1.6.1 — Data Subject Rights

Users have the following rights regarding their personal data:

Right to Access:

  • Users can request a copy of their personal data in our system
  • Administrators can download student records, attendance, grades in CSV/PDF format
  • Parents can view their child's data through the parent portal

Right to Correction:

  • Users can update their information (name, contact details, address)
  • Administrators can edit student records, correct grades, update attendance
  • Incorrect data can be reported to support@smartshala.local

Right to Deletion (With Limitations):

  • Users can request deletion of their account and associated data
  • Exceptions: Academic records, fee receipts, and official documents are retained per education board requirements (typically 10 years post-graduation)
  • Student data cannot be deleted until they exit the school; historical data is retained in archives
  • Parents must request through school administration; deletion is processed within 30 days

Right to Data Portability:

  • Users can request a complete export of their data in standard formats (CSV, PDF, JSON)
  • Administrators can export student lists, attendance records, fee details, marks
  • Data export is provided within 7 business days

Right to Withdraw Consent:

  • Users can opt out of non-essential data processing (analytics, marketing emails)
  • Withdrawing consent will not affect essential service functionality
  • Students/parents can opt out of WhatsApp notifications; notifications will switch to SMS/email

Right to Restrict Processing:

  • Users can request suspension of data processing for specific purposes
  • However, we must continue processing for legal obligations and service delivery
  • Requests are reviewed and implemented within 15 days

1.6.2 — School Administration Control

Schools (administrators) have full control over:

  • Who can access student data (role-based permissions)
  • What data is collected (optional fields for photo, medical info)
  • Communication preferences (enable/disable WhatsApp, SMS)
  • Data retention policies (how long records are kept)
  • Export and backup schedules
  • Third-party integrations (which external services are connected)

1.9 Children's Privacy

1.9.1 — Age Restrictions

SmartShala is designed for educational institutions serving children aged 5–25. Direct data collection from children is limited:

Student Accounts:

  • Students under 16 should NOT independently create accounts
  • School administrators create student accounts and manage credentials
  • Parents receive login credentials for parent portal
  • Students access the system only through school-authorized accounts

Parental Consent:

  • Guardians provide written/digital consent for data collection when school enrollment occurs
  • Schools must obtain explicit consent for sensitive data (medical info, photos)
  • Parents can withdraw consent at any time; student data collection stops immediately

1.9.2 — Special Protections for Minors

  • No advertising or marketing to students
  • No student data is shared with third parties for marketing
  • Student communication is limited to academic use (grades, assignments, attendance)
  • Parent portal ensures guardians can monitor all interactions
  • FERPA compliance (if applicable in US schools) — student education records are protected

If your school operates in the EU or serves EU residents:

  • GDPR Compliance: We comply with GDPR article 44+ requirements for international data transfers
  • Standard Contractual Clauses: Our data processors are bound by SCCs
  • Adequacy Assessment: Data is treated with equivalent security standards
  • User Rights: EU residents have additional rights under GDPR (including Right to Be Forgotten, with academic record exceptions)

If your school operates in other countries:

  • Data Localization: We respect country-specific data residency requirements
  • Local Law Compliance: We comply with education data protection laws (FERPA, state privacy laws, etc.)
  • Custom Agreements: Schools can request data to be stored in specific regions
